Alvian Bagus Agatha, S,Pd., M.Pd.

Alvian Bagus Agatha, S.Pd., M.Pd., is a doctoral student in Mathematics Education at the Faculty of Teacher Training and Education (FKIP), University of Jember. He completed his undergraduate studies at FKIP, University of Jember (FKIP UNEJ) in 2019. He is currently serving as a Research Assistant at PUI-PT CGANT, Universitas Jember. His research interests are Combinatorics Education, Graph Theory, and Machine Learning.

CGANT Workshop on AI

The CGANT Workshop on AI was held at Universitas Jember, focusing on the advancements of artificial intelligence in mathematics research. The workshop attracted experts and researchers who shared insights on how AI can be integrated with graph theory and combinatorics.
